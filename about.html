This is our team of travelling roadtrippers

Our unique blog covers the wonderful “off the beaten path” places to visit and our unique database contains the most interesting locations in the United States. Roadtrippin’ USA helps people discover this beautiful country with engaging articles that gives you an idea about what to do and what to plan for.

Annie Allen

As a photojournalist with a passion for outdoor adventure, she loves yoga, hiking and running with the wolves in the wild Southwest. What’s at the crossroads of art and reality? One of Natalie’s beautiful travel photographs.

Beth Jennings

Beth is a freelance photographer located in the depths of the Rocky Mountains. Growing up in Idaho and Wyoming, she has an immense admiration for the West and the people that inhabit the landscape. Her work centers around telling the stories where humans and nature intersect.

Rick McGregor

Rick lives in Southern California with his wife, three kids, dog, turtle and fish. He co-owns a photography and styling company called Dulcet Creative, and loves working alongside brands to bring their products and visions to life through images. He loves to travel, fix up his home, and explore.

Cory Parks

Cory Parks is a Bay Area writer specializing in stories about travel, history, and food. His work has appeared in Smithsonian Magazine, NPR, Atlas Obscura, Fodor’s Travel, and elsewhere.
